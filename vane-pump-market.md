<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vane pump market is commonly categorized into Type I and Type II markets based on design and application. Type I pumps are primarily used in low-pressure applications, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. They rely on a rotating vanes mechanism to create a fluid flow. In contrast, Type II pumps are designed for high-pressure environments, featuring enhanced materials and designs to withstand greater demands. These pumps are suited for more complex systems requiring precise fluid control and higher efficiency.</p></p>
