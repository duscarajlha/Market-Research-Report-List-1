<p><ul><li>Genetic Humanized Mouse Models</li><li>Cell-based Humanized Mouse Models</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humanized mouse model market is primarily categorized into two types: genetic and cell-based models. Genetic humanized mouse models are engineered to carry human genes, organs, or tissues, allowing for the study of human-specific diseases and drug responses. In contrast, cell-based humanized mouse models involve the transplantation of human cells or tissues into immunocompromised mice, enabling researchers to investigate immune responses, cancer development, and therapeutic efficacy in a more controlled human-like environment. Both models are essential for advancing biomedical research.</p></p>
