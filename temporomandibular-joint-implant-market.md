<p><p>The Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) implant market is primarily divided into two types: the Mandibular Component and the Fossa Component. The Mandibular Component is designed to replace or support the lower jaw, providing stability and function to patients with TMJ disorders. On the other hand, the Fossa Component addresses the upper joint cavity, ensuring proper alignment and movement of the jaw. Both components aim to alleviate pain and restore the range of motion, enhancing the quality of life for affected individuals.</p></p>
