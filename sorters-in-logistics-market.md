<p><p>Sorters in logistics are essential systems that enhance efficiency in sorting and distributing products. Flat sorters utilize horizontal trays to transport items, suitable for lightweight goods. Cross belt sorters employ a series of belts to redirect packages at various angles, ideal for diverse item sizes. Tilt tray sorters feature trays that tilt to drop items into designated areas, beneficial for mixed product types. Other sorter types may include pocket sorters and automated systems, each tailored to specific operational needs and space constraints.</p></p>
