<p><p>Radiation processing in the food industry involves using ionizing radiation to enhance food safety and shelf life. Gamma-based processing utilizes gamma rays emitted from radioactive isotopes, offering deep penetration for effectively reducing pathogens and spoilage organisms. In contrast, accelerator-based methods employ high-energy electron beams or X-rays generated by particle accelerators, allowing for rapid processing and flexibility in treatment levels. Both techniques are pivotal in extending product longevity, improving hygiene, and maintaining nutritional quality, catering to varying market needs.</p></p>
