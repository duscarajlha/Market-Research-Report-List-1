Oscillators generate stable signal frequencies based on SAW resonators, ensuring accurate timing in devices. Resonators enhance signal quality and performance. Transducers convert electrical signals to acoustic waves and vice versa, enabling communication between electronic and physical worlds. Other market segments include customized applications and advanced sensor technologies.</p></p>
