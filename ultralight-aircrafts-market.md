<p><p>The ultralight aircraft market encompasses three primary types: fixed-wing, flex-wing, and rotary-wing ultralight aircraft. Fixed-wing ultralights feature rigid wings, offering greater speed and stability, ideal for longer flights. Flex-wing ultralights possess flexible wings, allowing for easier maneuverability and gentle landings, making them popular for recreational flying. Rotary-wing ultralights include helicopters and autogyros, known for vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, providing unique operational flexibility. Each type serves distinct needs within the ultralight aviation community, catering to various pilot preferences and applications.</p></p>
