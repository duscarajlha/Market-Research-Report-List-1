<p><p>Job search engines operate in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based job search engines are hosted on the internet, offering accessibility from anywhere, easy integration with other systems, and scalability for users. They typically operate on a subscription model. In contrast, on-premises job search engines are installed locally on user systems, providing greater control over data and security. However, they require more maintenance and upfront investment, appealing to organizations with specific compliance or customization needs.</p></p>
