<p><p>The gastric bands market comprises two main types: adjustable and non-adjustable gastric bands. Adjustable gastric bands, designed to be tightened or loosened via an external port, allow for personalized weight loss management by altering the size of the stomach opening. In contrast, non-adjustable gastric bands, which are fixed in size once implanted, provide a consistent restriction without the ability for adjustments. Together, these options cater to diverse patient needs and preferences in the pursuit of effective weight management.</p></p>
