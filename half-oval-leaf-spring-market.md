<p><p>The half oval leaf spring market consists of two primary types: single leaf springs and multi-leaf springs. Single leaf springs are characterized by a single flat strip of steel, offering simplicity and lightweight design, making them suitable for lighter vehicles and applications. In contrast, multi-leaf springs comprise several layers of metal strips stacked together, providing enhanced load capacity and better stability, commonly used in heavier vehicles like trucks and trailers. Each type serves distinct performance requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
