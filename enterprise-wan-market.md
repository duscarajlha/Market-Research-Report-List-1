<p><p>The Enterprise WAN market encompasses two main types: Traditional WAN and SD-WAN. Traditional WAN relies on dedicated leased lines, such as MPLS, offering reliable but costly connectivity suited for stable environments. In contrast, SD-WAN utilizes software-defined technology to optimize network traffic over diverse connections, like broadband and LTE, providing flexibility, cost-effectiveness, and improved performance. SD-WAN enhances application-aware routing and scalability, making it ideal for organizations seeking agile, responsive networking solutions in an increasingly cloud-centric landscape.</p></p>
