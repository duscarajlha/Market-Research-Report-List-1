<p><p>The Porous Silicon-based Anode Material market includes two main types: SiO/C and Si/C. SiO/C combines silicon oxide with carbon, enhancing conductivity and stability while facilitating high-capacity lithium-ion storage. This blend mitigates the volume expansion challenges typical of silicon. Si/C, on the other hand, integrates silicon with a carbon matrix, promoting improved electron conductivity and mechanical integrity. Both materials aim to improve the performance and lifecycle of lithium-ion batteries, making them essential for advancements in energy storage technologies.</p></p>
