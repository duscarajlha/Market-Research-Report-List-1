<p><p>The rocket propulsion market is segmented into three main types: solid propulsion, liquid propulsion, and hybrid propulsion. Solid propulsion uses a solid fuel that ignites to produce thrust, offering simplicity and reliability. Liquid propulsion, on the other hand, employs liquid fuels and oxidizers, allowing for greater control and efficiency in performance. Hybrid propulsion combines elements of both, utilizing a solid fuel with a liquid oxidizer, balancing the advantages of each type. These variations cater to diverse applications in space exploration and satellite launches.</p></p>
