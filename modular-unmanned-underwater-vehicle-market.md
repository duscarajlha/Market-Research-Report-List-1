<p><p>The Modular Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (UUV) market comprises two main types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs are self-sufficient and programmed for various tasks, making them suitable for tasks like oceanographic research and environmental monitoring. In contrast, ROVs are tethered to a surface vessel and are operated by an onboard pilot, primarily used for inspection, repairs, and exploration in deeper waters. Both types enhance underwater operations in diverse industries, including defense, oil and gas, and research.</p></p>
