<p><p>The adsorption molecular sieves market is categorized primarily into A Type and X Type. A Type molecular sieves, typically made from zeolite A, are effective in adsorbing smaller molecules like water and can separate gases based on size. X Type molecular sieves, on the other hand, have a larger pore size suitable for adsorbing larger molecules, making them ideal for applications such as gas drying and separation. Both types are critical in industries such as petrochemical, pharmaceuticals, and environmental engineering.</p></p>
