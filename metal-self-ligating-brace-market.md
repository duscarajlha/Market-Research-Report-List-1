<p><strong>The Metal Self-ligating Brace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal self-ligating brace market is divided into two main types: active and passive. Active self-ligating braces utilize a spring-loaded mechanism that applies continuous force to the wire, promoting efficient tooth movement. In contrast, passive self-ligating braces allow the wire to slide freely within the bracket, minimizing friction and making adjustments easier. Both types offer distinct advantages, such as reduced treatment time and improved patient comfort, catering to different orthodontic needs and preferences.</p></p>
