<p><p>The rubber running track market is primarily divided into two types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber, derived from latex, offers excellent resilience and shock absorption, making it popular for recreational tracks. In contrast, synthetic rubber, often made from polymers, provides enhanced durability, weather resistance, and lower maintenance, making it ideal for professional and competitive environments. Each type caters to different user needs, with natural rubber favored for its eco-friendliness and comfort, while synthetic rubber appeals to high-performance requirements.</p></p>
