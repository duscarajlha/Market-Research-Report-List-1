<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pyrogen Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LAL Test</li><li>Chromogenic Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Test</li><li>Gel Clot Test</li><li>In Vitro Pyrogen Test</li><li>Rabbit Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pyrogen Testing Market encompasses various methods to detect pyrogens, including the Limulus Amebocyte Lysate (LAL) Test, which uses lysate from horseshoe crab blood. The Chromogenic Test detects endotoxins based on color changes, while the Turbidimetric Test measures cloudiness as an indicator of endotoxin presence. The Gel Clot Test forms a gel in response to endotoxins. In Vitro Pyrogen Testing involves cell-based assays, and the Rabbit Test, though less common, uses live rabbits to assess fever responses. Each method serves specific regulatory and research needs.</p></p>
