<p><strong>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically Steered</li><li>Mechanically Steered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial flat panel antennas market can be categorized into two main types: electronically steered and mechanically steered antennas. Electronically steered antennas utilize advanced technology, such as phased arrays, to adjust their direction electronically, enabling rapid and precise beam steering. In contrast, mechanically steered antennas rely on physical movements or pivots to change their orientation, which generally offers broader coverage but may be slower to respond. Both types cater to various applications, including satellite communications and wireless networks.</p></p>
