<p><strong>The Anti-spasmodic Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antimuscarinics</li><li>Smooth Muscle Relaxants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-spasmodic drugs market is primarily divided into two types: antimuscarinics and smooth muscle relaxants. Antimuscarinics, such as atropine, work by blocking acetylcholine at muscarinic receptors, reducing gastrointestinal motility and alleviating spasms. Smooth muscle relaxants, like hyoscine butylbromide, target the smooth muscle tissues directly, inducing relaxation and relieving cramping. Both categories are essential in managing conditions like irritable bowel syndrome and gastrointestinal disorders, contributing to a significant segment in the overall pharmaceutical market.</p></p>
