<p><p>The poultry insurance market comprises two main types: Non-Commercial Mortality Insurance and Commercial Mortality Insurance. Non-Commercial Mortality Insurance typically caters to small-scale or backyard poultry owners, providing coverage against unexpected deaths due to disease or accidents without the intention of large-scale production. In contrast, Commercial Mortality Insurance is designed for large-scale poultry operations, covering significant financial losses from mortality events in commercial flocks, ensuring business continuity and financial stability for producers.</p></p>
