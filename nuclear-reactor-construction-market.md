<p><p>The nuclear reactor construction market encompasses various types, primarily boiling water reactors (BWR) and pressurized water reactors (PWR). BWRs produce steam directly in the reactor core, facilitating electricity generation and simplifying design. PWRs, on the other hand, use a secondary circuit for steam production, allowing for higher pressure and temperature control. Other reactor types include heavy water reactors, fast breeder reactors, and small modular reactors, catering to diverse energy needs, technological advancements, and safety considerations in nuclear energy generation.</p></p>
