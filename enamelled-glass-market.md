<p><ul><li>Transparent</li><li>Translucent</li><li>Opaque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enamelling glass market is categorized into three primary types: transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent enamelled glass allows light to pass through while maintaining clarity, making it ideal for decorative applications and windows. Translucent enamelled glass diffuses light, providing privacy while still allowing some illumination, commonly used in shower doors and partitions. Opaque enamelled glass blocks light entirely, offering complete privacy and is often utilized in signage and architectural elements where visibility is not desired. Each type serves distinct aesthetic and functional purposes.</p></p>
