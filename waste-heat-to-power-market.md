<p><p>Waste Heat to Power technologies convert excess heat from industrial processes or power plants into usable electricity, enhancing energy efficiency. The Steam Rankine Cycle utilizes high-temperature steam to drive turbines, ideal for large-scale applications. Organic Rankine Cycles use organic fluids with lower boiling points, making them effective for lower temperature waste heat sources. The Kalina Cycle employs a mixture of water and ammonia, providing higher efficiency at varying temperatures. Each cycle is suited to different operational conditions, optimizing energy recovery.</p></p>
