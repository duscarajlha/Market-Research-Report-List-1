<p><strong>The Additive Manufacturing with Metal Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Additive Manufacturing with metal powders encompasses various techniques, primarily including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses a high-powered laser to selectively fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with precision. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum for similar fusion, often suited for high-performance applications. Other methods, such as Directed Energy Deposition (DED) and Binder Jetting, also contribute to the market, each offering unique benefits in terms of speed, material compatibility, and resolution.</p></p>
