<p><p>The cold chain monitoring market features three primary sensor types: temperature sensors, humidity sensors, and hybrid sensors. Temperature sensors track ambient conditions to ensure perishable goods remain within safe limits. Humidity sensors monitor moisture levels, crucial for products sensitive to humidity changes, such as pharmaceuticals and food items. Hybrid sensors combine both temperature and humidity monitoring capabilities, providing comprehensive environmental oversight. Together, these sensors play a vital role in maintaining the integrity of cold chain logistics, ensuring product safety and compliance.</p></p>
