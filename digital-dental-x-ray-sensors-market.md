<p><strong>The Digital Dental X-Ray Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-rays</li><li>Extraoral X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital dental X-ray sensors are classified into two primary types: intraoral and extraoral X-rays. Intraoral X-rays capture images from inside the mouth, providing detailed views of individual teeth and surrounding structures, crucial for diagnosing cavities, infections, and other dental issues. Extraoral X-rays, on the other hand, capture images from outside the mouth, such as panoramic and cephalometric views, helping in assessing overall dental health and planning for orthodontic treatments or surgical procedures. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and patient comfort.</p></p>
