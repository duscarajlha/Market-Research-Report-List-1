<p><p>Laser lithotripsy systems in urology are classified primarily into two types: holmium laser and thulium laser systems. Holmium lasers utilize high-energy pulses to fragment kidney stones, offering minimal tissue damage and effective hemostasis, making them a preferred choice for larger stones. Thulium lasers produce shorter wavelengths, providing precision and versatility, ideal for both stone fragmentation and soft tissue procedures. Both technologies enhance patient outcomes and are pivotal in minimally invasive urological procedures.</p></p>
