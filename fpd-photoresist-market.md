<p><p>The FPD (Flat Panel Display) photoresist market encompasses various types tailored for different display technologies. RGB photoresists facilitate color filter production, vital for vibrant display imagery. BM (Black Matrix) photoresists create the black layers that enhance contrast and reduce light leakage in displays.
