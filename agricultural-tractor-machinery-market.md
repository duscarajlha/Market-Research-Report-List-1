<p><p>The agricultural tractor machinery market primarily includes two types: wheel tractors and crawler tractors. Wheel tractors are versatile and suited for various terrains, offering higher speed and maneuverability on paved or soft ground, making them ideal for farming tasks like plowing and tilling. In contrast, crawler tractors, with their tracked design, provide excellent traction and stability on uneven, muddy, or rocky terrains, excelling in tasks such as land clearing and heavy-duty hauling, where grip and power are essential.</p></p>
