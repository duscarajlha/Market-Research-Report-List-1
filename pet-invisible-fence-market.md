<p><p>The pet invisible fence market encompasses two primary types: wireless fences and in-ground fences. Wireless fences utilize a transmitter to create a defined boundary, allowing pets freedom within a specific area while preventing them from crossing predetermined limits. In contrast, in-ground fences involve burying a wire along the property lines, transmitting signals to a receiver collar worn by the pet. Both systems aim to enhance pet safety and freedom while minimizing the visual impact of traditional fencing solutions.</p></p>
