<p><p>A Home Energy Management System (HEMS) optimizes energy use in residential settings by integrating various devices for monitoring, control, and automation. Key market types include ZigBee, a low-power mesh network ideal for device communication; Wi-Fi, offering high-speed connectivity for real-time data access; Z-Wave, designed for home automation with low energy consumption; HomePlug, utilizing existing electrical wiring for connectivity; and Wireless M-Bus, tailored for utility meter communication. Each technology provides unique benefits for efficient energy management.</p></p>
