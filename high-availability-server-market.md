<p><p>The High-Availability Server Market is categorized into five levels based on redundancy and uptime. </p><p>- **Level 1** offers basic redundancy with single points of failure, suitable for non-critical applications.  </p><p>- **Level 2** includes redundant components but may have manual failover, providing moderate uptime.  </p><p>- **Level 3** features automated failover and enhanced redundancy for critical applications.  </p><p>- **Level 4** ensures high redundancy and minimal downtime, often used by enterprises.  </p><p>- **Level 5** offers almost continuous availability with no planned downtime, crucial for mission-critical systems.</p></p>
