<p><p>Polyethylene Low Density (LDPE) is a highly flexible plastic, commonly used in packaging, containers, and various applications due to its excellent chemical resistance and durability. The LDPE market is primarily categorized into two production methods: the autoclave process and the tubular process. The autoclave process involves polymerization in large reactors under high pressure, resulting in high-quality LDPE. In contrast, the tubular process uses a continuous flow system, allowing for higher production rates and better efficiency, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
