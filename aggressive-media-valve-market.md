<p><p>The aggressive media valve market is primarily segmented into two main types: 2-way valves and 3-way valves. 2-way valves facilitate a straightforward flow control, allowing media to flow in one direction or be completely shut off, making them ideal for on-off applications. In contrast, 3-way valves manage the flow of media from multiple directions, enabling mixing, diverting, or regulating flows in complex systems. Both types are essential in industries handling aggressive substances, ensuring efficiency and safety in operations.</p></p>
