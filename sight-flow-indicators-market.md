<p><p>Sight flow indicators are crucial in monitoring fluid flow in pipelines, available in several types. Rotary indicators use a rotating vane to visually indicate flow direction and speed. Flapper indicators utilize a hinged flap that moves with fluid flow. Flutter indicators employ a flexible mechanism that vibrates with flow, providing a dynamic visual cue. Ball indicators feature a floating ball that moves with the fluid, indicating flow presence. Drip indicators monitor slow flow by capturing droplets, essential for processes requiring precision.</p></p>
