<p><p>Cloud gaming encompasses two primary market types: video streaming and file streaming. </p><p>In video streaming, games are processed on remote servers and streamed directly to the player's device, allowing instant play without downloads. This model typically requires a stable internet connection and provides seamless gaming experiences. </p><p>File streaming, on the other hand, involves temporarily downloading game files, enabling users to enjoy even high-fidelity games with reduced lag. Both methods allow players access to games across various devices without hardware limitations.</p></p>
