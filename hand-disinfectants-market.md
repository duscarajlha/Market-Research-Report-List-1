<p><p>The hand disinfectants market is primarily divided into two types: alcohol-based and non-alcohol types. Alcohol-based disinfectants, containing ethanol or isopropanol, are effective against a wide range of pathogens and tend to evaporate quickly, leaving hands feeling clean. Non-alcohol types, which may use ingredients like chlorhexidine or hydrogen peroxide, often cater to sensitive skin and are preferred in environments where alcohol is restricted. Each type serves distinct consumer needs while promoting hygiene and infection control.</p></p>
