<p><p>The plastic 3D printers market primarily consists of two key types: FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) and PolyJet photopolymer resin printers. FDM printers utilize thermoplastic filaments, melting and extruding them layer by layer to create objects. They are favored for their affordability and versatility. In contrast, PolyJet printers use liquid photopolymer resins cured by ultraviolet light, enabling high-resolution and intricate designs. This technology is ideal for producing detailed prototypes and complex geometries, appealing to industries requiring precision and aesthetic quality.</p></p>
