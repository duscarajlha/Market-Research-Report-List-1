<p><strong>The Recirculating Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop Type</li><li>Closed-Loop Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recirculating coolers are essential for maintaining optimal temperatures in various industrial processes. The market is segmented into two main types: open-loop and closed-loop systems. Open-loop coolers draw water from a source, cool it, and return it to the environment, making them suitable for applications with abundant water supply. Closed-loop coolers, on the other hand, circulate a coolant through a closed system, minimizing water usage and contamination, making them ideal for sensitive operations and areas with water scarcity.</p></p>
