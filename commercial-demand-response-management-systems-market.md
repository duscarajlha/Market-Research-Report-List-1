<p><p>Commercial Demand Response Management Systems (DRMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: conventional and automated. Conventional DRMS relies on manual processes where businesses adjust their energy consumption in response to peak demand periods, often through notifications from grid operators. In contrast, automated DRMS utilizes advanced technology to automatically manage and adjust energy usage based on real-time signals and analytics, enhancing efficiency and responsiveness to grid conditions without significant human intervention. Both systems aim to optimize energy use and reduce costs.</p></p>
