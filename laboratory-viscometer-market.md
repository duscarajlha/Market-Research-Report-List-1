<p><ul><li>In-line Process Viscometers</li><li>Portable Viscometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory viscometer market comprises mainly two types: in-line process viscometers and portable viscometers. In-line process viscometers are integrated into production lines, providing real-time viscosity measurements for consistent quality control. They are ideal for continuous monitoring in industrial applications. Portable viscometers, on the other hand, are handheld devices designed for easy transport and on-the-spot viscosity testing. They are commonly used in labs and field applications for quick assessments and troubleshooting without the need for stationary equipment.</p></p>
