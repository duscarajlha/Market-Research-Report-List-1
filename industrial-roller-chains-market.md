<p><p>The industrial roller chains market is primarily divided into single strand and multiple strand types. Single strand chains feature one continuous loop of links and are ideal for applications requiring simple mechanical motion and lower load capacities. In contrast, multiple strand chains consist of two or more parallel strands, enhancing strength and durability for heavy-duty applications. They are used in demanding environments where higher load-bearing capacity and reliability are essential, making them suitable for industries like manufacturing and material handling.</p></p>
