<p><p>Electrosurgical Generator Units (ESUs) are devices used in surgical procedures to cut tissue and coagulate blood by using high-frequency electrical currents. The market is segmented into three types: Monopole Electrosurgical Generators, which use a single active electrode that disperses the electrical current through the patient's body; Bipolar Electrosurgical Generators, featuring two electrodes that minimize current travel through tissues; and Vessel Sealing Generators, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels effectively while reducing thermal damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
