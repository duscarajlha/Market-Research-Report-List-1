<p><p>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) systems are classified primarily into three types: hollow fiber, flat sheet, and multi-tubular membranes. Hollow fiber membranes consist of fine strands bundled together, offering high surface area and efficient space utilization, making them popular in wastewater treatment. Flat sheet membranes provide a larger surface area for filtration and are easier to clean, while multi-tubular configurations enhance flux and mechanical strength. Each type presents unique advantages for specific applications in water treatment and resource recovery processes.</p></p>
