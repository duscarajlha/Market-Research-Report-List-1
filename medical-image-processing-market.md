<p><p>The medical image processing market encompasses various imaging modalities that aid in diagnosis and treatment. CT scans utilize X-rays to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body. X-rays are primarily used for visualizing bones and detecting abnormalities. Ultrasound employs sound waves to produce real-time images, particularly useful in obstetrics and cardiology. MRI uses magnetic fields and radio waves for detailed soft tissue imaging. Nuclear imaging involves tracer materials to visualize organ function. Other modalities may include fluoroscopy and endoscopy, each serving specialized medical needs.</p></p>
