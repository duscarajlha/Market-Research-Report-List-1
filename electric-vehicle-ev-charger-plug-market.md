<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) charger plug market is primarily divided into two types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current) chargers. DC chargers provide rapid charging, converting AC power from the grid to DC for immediate use, making them ideal for fast charging stations. Conversely, AC chargers supply power directly in its native form, suitable for home or public charging solutions, and are generally slower. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing speed, infrastructure requirements, and user convenience in EV adoption.</p></p>
