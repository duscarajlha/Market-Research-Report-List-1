<p><p>The offshore wind power cable market is segmented by voltage levels, reflecting the different needs for transmitting electricity from wind farms to the grid. Those between 35kV and 110kV are suited for medium distances, while 110kV to 220kV cables are designed for higher capacity transmission. Cables rated 220kV to 500kV handle long-distance transport, and those over 500kV are utilized for the most extensive offshore wind projects, supporting energy efficiency and stability.</p></p>
