<p><strong>The Automotive Recorder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive recorder market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel recorders capture data from one source, typically focusing on essential driving metrics, making them cost-effective and suitable for personal use. In contrast, multi-channel recorders gather data from multiple sources simultaneously, providing a comprehensive view of vehicle performance, safety, and driver behavior. These systems are often utilized in commercial fleets and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) for enhanced monitoring and analytics, leading to improved decision-making and safety outcomes.</p></p>
