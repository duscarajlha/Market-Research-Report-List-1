<p><p>The Uterine Tamponade System market consists of two primary types: single balloon and double balloon devices. Single balloon systems utilize a single inflatable balloon that is inserted into the uterus to exert pressure and control bleeding, often used for immediate management of postpartum hemorrhage. In contrast, double balloon systems employ two balloons, one positioned in the uterus and the other in the cervix, providing more effective stabilization and control of uterine atony. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes in critical situations.</p></p>
